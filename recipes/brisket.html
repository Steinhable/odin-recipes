Sixteen Hour Brisket

Slow smoked to perfection

Slow and low, til the crust is hard and the meat is tender

Ingredients List

  • Brisket
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Salt
  • Espresso
  • Butter

  1. Trim the larger areas of fat from brisket
  2. Set smoker to 200 degrees
  3. Blend togehter salt, pepper, garlic salr, and espresso
  4. Submerger briket in rub, thoroughly covering
  5. Check the smoker has reached 200 degrees
  6. Place brisket on smoker
  7. Check in every four hours for temperature
  8. Around 16 hours of cooking the internal temp will be about 205
  9. Remove brisket, slab on hefty amount of butter
  10. Wrap in tin foil and let rest at room temp for 30 min
  11. Remove from tinfoil, slice and enjoy
