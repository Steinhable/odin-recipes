Famous Texas Burger

A Declicious Texas Style Burger

Take your taste buds south of the mason dixie line with this delicious texas style smash burger

Ingredients List

  • Beef Patties
  • Onions
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Sesame Seed Bun
  • MayoChup

  1. Take beef patties out 20 minutes prior to cook
  2. Slice onions, looking for strips
  3. Set heat on blackstone or flat top to high
  4. Roll beef patties into ball
  5. Build on blackstone
  6. Place burger ball and top with onion strips
  7. With the butt of spatula firmly press ball down
  8. While patties searing, grab buns
  9. Slather buns with mayochup, place on blackstone
  10. Flip burger patties
  11. Remove buns and set on plate
  12. Place 4-6 pickles on bottom bun
  13. Place cheddar cheese on each patty
  14. Stack burgers and place on bottom bun
  15. Place top bun
