Nashville Chicken

Heat even Hattie Mae would want

Crispy, tender, juciy chicken kicked up from Nashville

Ingredients List

  • Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • ButterMilk
  • Butter
  • Brioche Bun
  • Canola Oil
  • Pickles
  • Coleslaw
  • Flour
  • Corn Flour

  1. Trim the fat from chicken
  2. Whisk together cayenne and buttermilk
  3. Place trimmed chicken in buttermilk blend
  4. Let sit it fridge for 12-24 hours
  5. Remove chicken from fridge 20 minutes before cook
  6. Put Canola oil in pot and heat
  7. Once oil is hot, remove chicken from buttermilk
  8. Whisk together flour and corn flour
  9. Lay chicken in flour blend and press firmly down
  10. Allow chicken to cook for 6-7 minutes
  11. Probe chicken for temperature between 165 and 175
  12. Place pickle on bottom bun
  13. Submerge chicken in butter and place on bun
  14. Top with cayenne pepper, coleslaw, and top bun
